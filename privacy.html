Privacy Policy

Alderney Ferry Services Limited · Company Registration No. 2038

Last updated: January 2026

Who we are

Alderney Ferry Services Limited ("we", "us", "our") operates passenger ferry crossings between Alderney, Guernsey and Cherbourg, France. Our registered address is 9 English Row, Braye Street, Alderney GY9 3XT.

Information we collect

When you make a booking, contact us, or use our website, we may collect:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Travel details, including dates, routes, and passenger numbers
  • Payment information, processed securely by our booking provider, Bookeo
  • Messages you send us via our contact form, email, or WhatsApp

How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Process and manage your ferry bookings
  • Communicate with you about your crossing, including delays or cancellations
  • Respond to enquiries and provide customer support
  • Meet our legal and regulatory obligations

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only where necessary — for example, with our booking platform (Bookeo) to process reservations, or with relevant authorities for passport and border control purposes on the Cherbourg route.

Data retention

We retain booking and contact information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, and to comply with our legal and accounting obligations.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To make a request, contact us at info@alderneyferry.com.

Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies required for booking functionality. We do not use cookies for third-party advertising.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at info@alderneyferry.com or 07781 11 97 96.
