How It Started

A lifeline,
not a business plan.

Alderney Ferry Services Limited is a family-owned enterprise established in 2022. Founders Dan and Charlie saw a gap in regional transport — no scheduled, reliable passenger link between Alderney and Guernsey — and set out to fix it themselves.

What started as a single route has grown into a proper lifeline service: a timetable built around giving islanders and visitors a full day on either side, a second vessel added for a direct France crossing, and a fishing charter business alongside it.

"We started this because we saw a gap — islanders and visitors needed a proper, reliable lifeline between Alderney and Guernsey. It's grown from there ever since."
— Dan & Charlie, Founders
