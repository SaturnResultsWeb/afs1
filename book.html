Check-in

Arrive 30 minutes before departure. Alderney: walled area near Alderney Sailing Club. Guernsey: inter-island pontoon. Cherbourg: Bureau du port de plaisance, Port Chantereyne.

Before You Book

Good to know.

Weather & Delays

Skippers monitor conditions constantly. If your sailing is affected, we'll notify you and offer a refund or rebooking.

Cancellations

Full refund minus £10pp per leg if cancelled 48+ hours before travel. No refund inside 48 hours.

Dogs & Bikes

Dogs welcome at £5 per leg, seated with you in the cabin. Bikes carried on deck for a small charge.

Luggage

No fixed limit — bring what you can manage yourself on and off the boat, free of charge.
